About Mind & Health Wellness Centre

Dedicated to healing your mind, body & soul through Hypnosis, Yoga and Ayurveda.

Our Founder: Dr. Haribhau Dorlikar

With over 26 years of experience in clinical and stage hypnosis, Dr. Dorlikar holds an MPhil & PhD in Hypnosis & Alternative Medicine. He has successfully treated over 70,000 patients through holistic wellness therapies.

Our center believes in the perfect blend of ancient healing with modern science. We aim to bring inner peace and physical well-being to every individual by combining Hypnotherapy, Yoga, Meditation and Ayurveda treatments.

To empower individuals to live a healthier, happier life by providing natural, holistic healing solutions that address both mind and body.
